3. Payment + booking + CRM integrations: cost reality

Rough, current ranges (US, online; you always tell clients to check live pricing):
	•	Stripe – default developer choice
	•	Typical: ~2.9% + $0.30 per successful online card transaction; ~3.1% + extra 1.5% cross-border on international; ACH is cheaper (~0.8% capped).

	•	Apple itself doesn’t add extra merchant fees; you pay whatever Stripe/Square/etc. charge, same as normal card transactions.
	•	PayPal
	•	Common online range: 2.99–3.49% + a fixed fee ($0.49) for most business transactions, with variations by product and volume.
	•	Cash App Business
	•	Around 2.6–2.75% per collected payment, no monthly fee; specific schedule depends on product mode and changes over time.
	•	Klarna / BNPL (Pay in 4, etc.)
	•	Merchant fees are noticeably higher, often in the 3–5.99% range plus a fixed fee, which merchants accept because conversion and AOV go up.
	•	HubSpot CRM
	•	Free tier: good enough for a lot of solopreneurs / micro SMBs.
	•	Paid: starts in the ~$15–20/user/month range and climbs sharply for marketing automation.
	•	Calendly
	•	Free personal tier; then ~$10–16/seat/month for most pro/team plans; enterprise from ~$15k/year.

So your value is not “I get you cheaper fees” — it’s:
