Serving New Bern, NC and remote clients

Whether your work comes from Nextdoor, word of mouth, Instagram, or a simple link you text to people, this flow keeps everything pointed at one place: your online front desk.

The goal is simple: fewer back-and-forth messages, fewer lost leads, and a calm, predictable way new clients show up on your calendar.

  • We map how people currently find and contact you.
  • We plug your channels into one clear “front desk” page.
  • We add form, booking, and payment in a way that fits your work.
  • We connect it to your email or CRM so nothing gets lost.
What this replaces
  • Loose DMs spread across Facebook, Nextdoor, and text.
  • “Sorry, I missed this” messages when life gets busy.
  • Paper notes with names, phone numbers, and requests.
  • Guessing what to charge or how to explain your offers.
