Book a 15-minute intro

This is a short, focused call to understand where you are, what you’re trying to fix or build, and whether I’m the right person to help.

What we’ll do on this call

  • Briefly map your current tools and biggest pain points.
  • Clarify what “one clear system” would look like for you.
  • Decide if it makes sense to start with a session or a build.

Good use of this call

  • You’re overwhelmed by scattered tools, DMs, and manual follow-ups.
  • You want a concrete, scoped system — not a vague “transformation”.
  • You care about doing things in a way that won’t cause trouble later.

What to prepare (optional)

  • Rough list of tools you currently use (email, CRM, scheduling, payment, etc.).
  • One or two examples of where things break or leak today.
  • Any hard constraints: budget, timeline, compliance/regulatory concerns.

This is not a high-pressure sales call. If it’s not a fit, I’ll say that and, if possible, point you to a better option.

Pick a time

If you don’t see the calendar, you can also open it directly in a new tab: calendly.com/plewak-jeff/intro or email plewak.jeff@gmail.com with a few times that work for you.
