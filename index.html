What I do (without jargon)

Simple, honest systems that support your work.

I don’t promise “infinite leads.” I help you set up one clear, trustworthy flow: from first click to booked work and follow-up.

Clarify your offers and intake

We write plain-language explanations of what you do, who it’s for, and what people can ask you for. Then we build intake forms that feel more like a friendly conversation than a government form.

Connect booking and payments

Calendly (or similar) for time, Stripe for payments, and one place where you, your team, or your clients can see what is coming up and what has been paid for.

Keep proof and records in order

Light, evidence-friendly exports: CSVs, PDFs, and simple diagrams that help you answer “what happened when?” without digging through screenshots.
