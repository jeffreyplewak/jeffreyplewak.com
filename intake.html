Step 1 · Client intake

Help me see how your business really works today.

This intake form is how I learn what you sell, how people currently reach you, and where the leaks are between “someone raises their hand” and “job done and paid.”

It’s meant for more detailed projects: full lead hubs, front desks, or custom development (legal, medical-adjacent, audit, hardware, or AI-heavy work). If you just want to say hello or ask a quick question, use the short contact form instead.

What I’m looking for

  • How you currently capture leads and messages.
  • What “booked work” looks like in your world.
  • Any constraints: legal, medical, compliance, hardware, etc.

How this is used

Your answers stay between us and are used only to evaluate fit, design a sensible plan, and prepare for a call or proposal. I don’t sell lists or data, and I don’t add you to any newsletter by default.

Don’t have time for a full intake?

No problem. You can:

  • Book a 15-minute intro call and we’ll fill this in together later.
  • Use the short contact form to send a quick note explaining your situation.

For complex projects (especially anything touching legal, medical, or audit), this intake helps us move faster and avoid missed details.
