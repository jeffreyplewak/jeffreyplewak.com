What happens with your answers

We turn messy notes into a clear, realistic first step.

The goal is not to impress me. The goal is to surface what is actually happening so we can design something that will hold up in real life.

1. I review and map your flow.

I go through your answers, sketch how leads move today, and note where messages or obligations are getting dropped or delayed.

2. You get a written summary.

You receive a short, plain-language summary of what I’m seeing, plus a few realistic options for what to do next.

3. We decide the smallest useful step.

Often that’s the $150 plan or a scoped build phase. If it’s not a fit, I’ll say that clearly and you still keep the summary.
