What you’re actually buying

You’re not paying for vague “hours”. You’re paying for a small, well-defined system that behaves predictably on good days and bad ones.

1

Clarity

We name the bottleneck, the outcome, and what “good enough” looks like in your world.

2

Design

We choose tools and flows that respect your time, risk, and energy — not just what’s trendy.

3

Build & test

We implement the system with real data, test edge cases, and sanity-check it on a busy week.

4

Proof & handoff

You leave with diagrams, notes, and clear “owners” so the system doesn’t depend on me long-term.

For clients and hiring managers

If you’re a client

Treat these packages as rails: they keep scope tight and predictable. We’ll confirm fit in a short call before anything is paid.

If you’re hiring

Use this page as a snapshot of how I think about systems work, constraints, and value. The numbers help calibrate seniority and scope.

If you’re unsure

Start with a Strategy Session. Worst case, you get a prioritized map of what to do next and a clearer sense of whether I’m the right fit.

Next step

If one of these packages feels right, we’ll lock scope in a 15-minute call and pick a start date. If you’re not sure, we’ll decide together whether a Strategy Session makes sense.
