Services

I work with small teams, solo practices, and serious creators to design and ship systems that reduce chaos, respect compliance, and still feel like yours.

1. Strategy & audit

We start by mapping what you already have: tools, workflows, data flows, and risks. This includes both technical and compliance angles when needed.

  • Inventory of tools, automations, and manual steps.
  • Clear list of bottlenecks, leaks, and failure points.
  • Short document summarizing options and trade-offs.
Book a strategy session

2. Build & automation

We pick one concrete outcome and ship it. No giant roadmaps; one real, testable change at a time that you can understand.

  • Funnels and microsites wired to your actual tools.
  • Automations for intake, scheduling, follow-ups, or hand-offs.
  • Documentation in plain language and simple diagrams if needed.
See build packages

3. Ongoing partnership

For teams or creators who want an architect on call instead of random one-off projects. We extend your systems carefully and keep them stable.

  • Light-weight retainer focused on outcomes, not hours.
  • Regular check-ins and prioritized improvements.
  • Guardrails for compliance and data handling baked in.
Discuss a retainer

Problem spaces I work in

  • Lead capture and follow-up that currently leaks everywhere.
  • Scattered tools: email, DMs, forms, spreadsheets, CRMs.
  • Creator funnels across platforms (Reddit, X, OF, etc.).
  • SMB operations that need record-keeping or audit trails.

What you can expect from me

  • Direct, honest communication — no fluff, no fake urgency.
  • Careful attention to compliance and data handling.
  • Systems you can actually own and modify later.
  • Clear decisions: “yes”, “no”, or “not yet” with reasons.
