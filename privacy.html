Privacy

How this site uses basic information and third-party services.

1. Website and contact

  • This site is a static marketing site. It does not run custom server-side code for tracking or profiling visitors.
  • You can contact Jeffrey Plewak directly by email at plewak.jeff@gmail.com or by using external tools linked from this site (for example, Calendly).

2. Information you choose to provide

  • If you send an email, book a call, or submit information through a third-party service, the details you provide (such as name, contact information, and project description) are used to respond to you and, if agreed, to deliver services.
  • Emails and related correspondence may be kept as records of work and communication.

3. Payments and third-party processors

  • Payments for many packages are handled through third-party providers such as Stripe, PayPal, or other processors. Those providers process your payment details on their own systems in line with their own terms and privacy policies.
  • This site does not store or process your card number or banking credentials directly.

4. Calendly and scheduling

  • Calendly may be used to let you schedule consultations. When you book, Calendly collects information you enter (such as name, email, and time zone) under its own privacy policy.
  • Scheduling data is used to coordinate calls and related work.

5. Cookies and analytics

  • The core HTML and CSS of this site do not require cookies to function.
  • Embedded third-party services (such as Calendly or payment providers) may set their own cookies or similar technologies in line with their policies.

6. Your choices

  • You can choose not to provide information through the site and instead contact directly by email.
  • If you would like to update or remove information you have provided directly (for example in email or Calendly notes), you can send a request to plewak.jeff@gmail.com.

7. Changes

This Privacy page may be updated over time. The version posted here is the current one. Continuing to use the site after any update means you accept the updated notice.
