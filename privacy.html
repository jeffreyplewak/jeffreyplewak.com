Privacy policy

This page explains what information is collected when you use jeffreyplewak.com, how it is used, and what choices and rights you may have.

This policy is written for clarity and transparency. It is not legal advice.

Effective date: 6 December 2025

1. Who operates this site

This website (jeffreyplewak.com) is operated by:

Jeffrey Plewak
Email: plewak.jeff@gmail.com
Location: New Bern, North Carolina, United States

For the purposes of many data protection laws (such as the EU/UK General Data Protection Regulation), I am the “data controller” for personal information collected through this site, except where third-party tools act as independent controllers under their own policies.

2. Scope of this policy

This policy covers personal information collected through:

  • This website and its pages (Home, Pricing, Book, Contact, Intake, legal pages).
  • Contact and intake forms embedded from services such as HubSpot.
  • Booking tools embedded from services such as Calendly.
  • Payment-related links or checkouts provided by Stripe, PayPal, or similar providers.
  • Analytics and logging tools used to understand how the site is used.

It does not cover information collected outside this site (for example, on Upwork, Fiverr, LinkedIn, or other platforms). Those services have their own privacy terms.

3. Information I collect

3.1 Information you provide directly

You may choose to provide personal information, for example when you:

  • Submit a contact form.
  • Fill out the client intake questionnaire.
  • Book a call through Calendly or similar tools.
  • Purchase a session or package and follow a payment link.

Depending on what you submit, this can include:

  • Identification and contact details (name, email address, phone number).
  • Business details (business name, role, location, website, services offered).
  • Project and system details (current tools, processes, problems, goals, constraints).
  • Scheduling details (availability, preferred times, time zone).
  • Payment-related information (for example, which package you select). Card and bank details are handled directly by payment providers, not stored on this site.

3.2 Information collected automatically

When you visit this site, certain technical information is collected automatically, for example:

  • IP address and approximate location (country or region).
  • Device and browser information (type, version, operating system).
  • Pages visited, time on page, links clicked, and similar usage data.
  • Date and time of your visit and referrer information (for example, a social link).

This data may be collected through server logs, first-party analytics, or third-party tools, and may use cookies or similar technologies. It is used to keep the site secure and to understand how people use it.

3.3 Information from third-party platforms

If you follow links to other platforms (for example, Upwork, Fiverr, LinkedIn, Calendly, HubSpot-hosted forms, or payment providers), those services may share limited information back, such as whether a booking or payment was completed. The details are governed by their own privacy policies.

4. How your information is used

I use the information described above to:

  • Operate, secure, and maintain this website.
  • Respond to messages, questions, and requests you send.
  • Schedule and run calls, sessions, and projects.
  • Provide services you request and keep basic records of the work.
  • Process payments and keep necessary accounting records.
  • Understand how people find and use the site to improve content and layout.
  • Comply with legal and regulatory obligations, such as tax and bookkeeping rules.

5. Legal bases for processing (EU/UK visitors)

If you are in the EU, EEA, UK, or another region where data protection laws require a legal basis for processing, I rely on one or more of the following, depending on the situation:

  • Contract: When processing is necessary to provide services you request (for example, running a session or project).
  • Legitimate interests: For activities such as securing the site, preventing abuse, understanding usage, and running a sustainable consulting practice, where these interests are not overridden by your rights and interests.
  • Consent: Where you actively agree to something optional (for example, certain cookies, or being contacted for a follow-up that is not strictly required).
  • Legal obligations: When processing is required to meet obligations such as tax and accounting rules.

6. Sharing and third-party services

I do not sell your personal information.

I may share or allow access to your information with third-party service providers who help run this site and business, for example:

  • Hosting and infrastructure providers (for example, the platform hosting this website and related assets).
  • Email and productivity tools used to send and receive messages and store project notes.
  • Contact and CRM tools such as HubSpot, which handle forms and follow-up communications.
  • Scheduling tools such as Calendly, used to book calls and sessions.
  • Payment providers such as Stripe or PayPal, which process payments on their own systems.
  • Analytics and monitoring tools used to understand site usage and keep it secure.

These providers are expected to process personal information only on documented instructions or under their own controller responsibilities, and to protect it appropriately. Their policies may give you additional choices and rights beyond what is described here.

7. Cookies and similar technologies

This site may use cookies and similar technologies (such as local storage or pixels) to:

  • Remember basic settings or choices you make (for example, cookie preferences).
  • Help embedded tools such as forms, booking widgets, and payment providers function.
  • Measure traffic and understand which pages and links are used.

The cookie banner and controls on this site give you an opportunity to accept or decline certain categories of cookies where applicable, consistent with current cookie and privacy guidance.

You can also manage cookies through your browser settings. Blocking or deleting cookies may affect some features (for example, embedded booking or payment tools).

8. How long information is kept

Personal information is kept only as long as reasonably necessary for the purposes described above, including:

  • To respond to your requests and maintain an ongoing working relationship.
  • To keep records of work done, where appropriate for quality, reference, or legal reasons.
  • To comply with tax, accounting, and other legal requirements that require certain records to be kept for a period of time.

When information is no longer needed, it may be deleted or anonymized, subject to technical and legal limits.

9. Your choices and rights

The rights available to you depend on where you live and which laws apply. In many cases, you may have some or all of the following:

  • Access: Ask for a copy of personal information held about you.
  • Correction: Ask to correct inaccurate or incomplete information.
  • Deletion: Ask to delete personal information, subject to legal obligations to keep certain records.
  • Restriction: Ask to limit how your information is used in certain circumstances.
  • Portability: Ask for certain information in a structured, machine-readable format where technically feasible.
  • Objection: Object to certain uses of your information, including some uses based on legitimate interests.
  • Withdraw consent: Where processing is based on consent, withdraw that consent at any time (this will not affect past processing carried out with your consent).

If you are a resident of a jurisdiction with specific rights (such as EU/EEA, UK, California, or other U.S. states with privacy laws), you may have additional or more detailed rights. Those laws may require specific processes for verifying your identity and handling requests.

To exercise any of these rights, or to ask questions, contact plewak.jeff@gmail.com. I will respond as required by applicable law.

10. Children’s privacy

This site and the services described on it are intended for adults and business users, not children. I do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 16. If you believe a child has provided personal information through this site, please contact me so that it can be reviewed and, where appropriate, removed.

11. How information is protected

Reasonable technical and organizational measures are used to help protect personal information, such as:

  • Using reputable hosting and infrastructure providers.
  • Using HTTPS to encrypt traffic between your browser and this site.
  • Limiting access to personal information to what is needed to provide services.

No website or system can guarantee perfect security, but the aim is to keep risks proportionate and to address issues if and when they are discovered.

12. Questions, concerns, and complaints

If you have any questions or concerns about this policy or how your information is handled, contact:

Email: plewak.jeff@gmail.com

Depending on where you live, you may also have the right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection or consumer privacy authority. For example, individuals in the UK can contact the Information Commissioner’s Office (ICO).

This policy may be updated from time to time to reflect changes in the site, services, or applicable laws. When it is updated, the “Effective date” at the top of this page will be changed. If the changes are significant, a more prominent notice may be provided on the site.
